Best Friends Animal Society

Mission Statement

"The mission of Best Friends Animal Society is to bring about a time when there are No More Homeless Pets. We do this by helping end the killing in America's animal shelters through building community programs and partnerships all across the nation. We believe that by working together we can Save Them All."

About

Best Friends Animal Society is a American nonprofit welfare organization located in Kanab, Utah. This organization has shelters, rescue groups and members to promote pet adoption, no-kill animal rescue, and spay-and-neuter practices. Best Friends' goal is to reach "no-kill" in this country by 2025.
